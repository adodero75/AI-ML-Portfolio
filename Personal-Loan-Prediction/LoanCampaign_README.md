# Personal Loan Campaign Prediction

## Business Problem
AllLife Bank wants to predict which liability customers are most likely to accept a personal loan offer.

## Solution Approach
Performed EDA, data preprocessing, built Decision Tree models with pre-pruning and post-pruning, evaluated performance.

## Techniques Used
Decision Tree Classifier, cost-complexity pruning, model evaluation (Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1-score).

## Key Insights
Income, education level, and credit card spending are top predictors of loan acceptance.

## Recommendations
Target high-income customers with graduate/professional education and high credit card spending; optimize marketing spend.
